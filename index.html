Explore Mirissa

The Pearl Of Southern Coast

Mirissa is a small coastal town in the Matara District of Sri Lanka's Southern Province. Located approximately 150 kilometres south of Colombo, it sits close to sea level and offers one of the country's most beautiful beach experiences.

Famous for its golden beaches, nightlife, whale watching and relaxed atmosphere, Mirissa has become a favourite destination for travelers from around the world.
