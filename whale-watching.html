Why Choose Mirissa for Whale Watching?

Located along the beautiful southern coast of Sri Lanka, Mirissa is internationally recognized as one of the best places in the world to witness the magnificent Blue Whale in its natural habitat. Every season, these gentle giants migrate through the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, creating unforgettable wildlife encounters just a short boat ride from shore.

Besides the world's largest mammal, visitors frequently encounter Sperm Whales, Bryde's Whales, Spinner Dolphins, Fin Whales, and many other fascinating marine species. With plenty of time for photography and wildlife observation, Tours Mirissa delivers a safe, comfortable, and unforgettable ocean adventure led by experienced local experts.
