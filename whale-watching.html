Why Choose Mirissa for Whale Watching?

Located along the beautiful southern coast of Sri Lanka, Mirissa is internationally recognized as one of the best places in the world to witness the magnificent Blue Whale in its natural habitat.

Visitors can also encounter Sperm Whales, Bryde's Whales, Spinner Dolphins, Fin Whales, and many other marine species. Tours Mirissa delivers a safe, comfortable, and unforgettable ocean adventure.
